University College of Engineering Arni(UCEA) is one of the constituent college of Anna University Chennai. It was establised in the year 2015. The college is located at Thatchur,Around 15km away from the Arni.The college is a co-educational institution , and it separate hostel facilities for boy and girls.The college currently offers five under graduate Programmes in Engineering with an intake of 60 students in a each branch and each medium of study viz,Civil Engineering(Tamil,English),Mechanical Engineering(Tamil,English),Computer Science Engineering, Electrical and Electronical Engineering,Electronical Communication Engineering.The students are adimitted in this institution through TNEA single window counselling conducted by Anna University.
